This dashboard answers how discounts impact both revenue and profitability by analyzing the relationship between gross sales, total orders, and profit margin. It helps identify whether increased sales are coming at the cost of reduced margins and supports optimizing discount strategies.


One modeling decision I made for this project was to create a dedicated Date table and link it to the Sales fact table. This enabled time intelligence functions like YoY growth and ensured correct chronological sorting. It also helped maintain a clean star schema, improving performance and scalability. Additionally, I created hierarchies and structured revenue and discount measures separately, which improved the overall report design and flexibility.
